// What we do

Built for people in back-to-back calls.

Stop frantically typing. Stop dreading the post-meeting cleanup. Lyrola handles the boring parts so you can stay present.

No bot in the call

Lyrola listens locally on your device. No "Notetaker" appearing in the participant list. No awkward client moments.

Works in every app

Zoom, Meet, Teams, phone speakers, even in-person. If sound comes out of your machine, Lyrola hears it.

Action items & summaries

Get clean action items and meeting summaries pulled straight from what was said — ready to share the moment the call ends.

Live meeting transcriptions

Watch the conversation transcribe itself in real time, color-coded by speaker. Searchable from the moment a word is spoken.

Ask anything, during or after

Ask an AI agent questions mid-call without breaking flow, or revisit any meeting later — Lyrola searches across every conversation you've had.

Made for fast typers

// How it works

Three things, in order.

01

Open Lyrola.

That's the whole setup. No calendar permissions, no Zoom integration, no bot to invite. The app launches with a single keystroke.

02

Take notes the lazy way.

Jot fragments. Misspell things. Use your own shorthand. Lyrola records audio in the background and lines it up with what you wrote.

03

Hit Generate. Send it.

Get a clean, shareable summary in seconds — with action items, decisions, and follow-ups extracted in your team's tone.
